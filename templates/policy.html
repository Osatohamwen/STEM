1. Program Overview

The Skills Training And Empowerment Ministry's Program is designed to equip members of the church community with practical, income-generating skills. It aims to promote self-reliance, personal development, and economic empowerment in alignment with biblical values and the mission of the church.

2. Available Skill Areas

  • Baking
  • Hairdressing
  • Solar Installation
  • Photography
  • Hair Tying

3. Eligibility

  • Must be a registered member of the church.
  • Must be at least 16 years old.
  • Must commit to completing the training program.
  • Should demonstrate a willingness to learn and abide by program guidelines.

4. General Rules and Conduct

  • Attendance: All trainees must attend at least 90% of scheduled classes. Habitual lateness or absenteeism may lead to dismissal.
  • Respect: Trainees must treat instructors, church leaders, and fellow trainees with respect.
  • Discipline: Unruly behavior, violence, or use of vulgar language will not be tolerated.
  • Dress Code: Trainees are expected to dress modestly and appropriately.
  • Use of Materials: All materials provided must be used responsibly and returned in good condition.

5. Code of Ethics

  • No exchange of money for admission into the program.
  • No form of discrimination or favoritism.
  • Confidentiality must be maintained in interpersonal issues.
  • All teachings must uphold moral and Christian values.

6. Attendance and Participation

  • Classes will run [Date and time are yet to be finalized.].
  • All absences must be communicated in advance with a valid reason.
  • Participation in all practical sessions is compulsory.

7. Certification

  • Participants who complete the training and meet attendance/assessment requirements will receive a certificate of completion.
  • The church reserves the right to withhold certification for non-compliance with rules.

8. Termination of Participation

  • Gross misconduct or repeated violation of rules.
  • Misuse of church property or training materials.
  • Disrespect to instructors or church leadership.

9. Participant Acknowledgment and Agreement

By enrolling in this program, participants acknowledge and accept all terms, rules, and regulations, and commit to fulfilling all program requirements to the best of their ability
